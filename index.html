About Our College

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

AVS Inter College has been a beacon of quality education in Jaunpur for over three decades. We provide a nurturing environment where every student is inspired to achieve their highest potential — from KG through Class XII.

Our institution blends modern pedagogy with timeless values — discipline, integrity, and a love for learning — to prepare students for a rapidly changing world.
